Reminder: Claim your Bankless Badge

You are eligible to claim a badge now if you subscribed before May 1, 2021

Ryan Sean Adams
May 23

Note: If you’re already claimed your 2021 Bankless Badge ignore this reminder! This message is for those Premium subscribers who have not yet claimed their 2021 badge.

Dear Bankless Nation,

A reminder…

Good things happen to those who claim their Bankless Badge! It’s important to claim your badge as soon as you are eligible so you don’t miss out on opportunities.

You are eligible to claim now if you subscribed before May 1, 2021…

If you subscribed before May 1, 2021 you should already have a Badge claim link in your email. To mint please check the following

  1. See if you already have the email. Search your spam folder for an email from lucas@banklesshq.com or a subject title “Claim your 2021 Bankless Badge”. Here’s what it looks like:

  1. Are you sure you’re eligible? If you became a Premium member after April 31st you’ll get a Badge on the next minting on June 1st. That means if you became a Premium subscriber in May don’t expect a minting email until June 1st!

  2. Have you already claimed? Maybe you claimed your 2021 badge already and don’t remember. Use the POAP scanner to see if you already hold the badge on xDAI or the Ethereum mainnet.

If after following the above steps you still don’t have a minting email please contact support@banklesshq.com ASAP and we’ll resend the link over the next few days.

If you subscribed on or after May 1, 2021 you’ll get a Badge minting link on June 1

If you subscribed on or after May 1, 2021 you will receive your badge minting email from “lucas@banklesshq.com” on June 1st. The email will include instructions to mint. We cannot send you a minting email before this date!

NOTE: No need to migrate your badge to mainnet! Save gas. Keep it on xDAI sidechain.

FAQ on the Badge…

What’s a Bankless Badge? An access NFT you hold on Ethereum or xDAI—info here.

Why are we doing this? Because we want everyone that’s a Premier subscriber to mint their Badge and start using it. Don’t wait. Mint. Every subscriber should have one.

Am I eligible to get a 2021 badge? If you were a premium subscriber anytime from January 1st 2021 to April 30th 2021 you should already have a email in your inbox from lucas@banklesshq.com with a unique link to mint your 2021 badge. If you became a subscriber after April 30th you have to wait until June 1st for your badge.

How is the Badge related to the Bankless DAO? The list of Bankless Badge holders was used by the Bankless DAO genesis team to distribute BANK tokens. So far only those who were eligible to receive a Badge prior to May 1st have been granted a distribution of BANK tokens. The latest developments on BANK token distribution can be found in announcement post and Proposal #3.

We mint all new badges on xDAI to save gas!

No need to migrate from xDAI to mainnet—enter raffles from xDAI.

Stay tuned to the newsletter for updates on badge perks and opportunities. 

Just make sure you have a badge!

- RSA
